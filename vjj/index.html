Common Inquiries

How do I verify the purity of my gold purchase?

Every gold item at V.J.J. Saraf comes with official BIS Hallmarked laser engraving, ensuring 22KT (916) or 24KT (999) purity as promised.

Do you provide custom design services?

Yes! We specialize in custom-making traditional Maharashtrian designs based on your specifications or heritage patterns you bring us.

What is the warranty on Men's Bentex jewellery?

Our premium Bentex collection comes with a durability guarantee on both the plating and the base metal strength under normal wear.
